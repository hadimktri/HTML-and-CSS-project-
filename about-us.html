about us

Certina Tile glazed stoneware tiles

Certina Tile is a company that invents, designs and makes premium quality ceramics - Italian tiles that are appreciated the world over and able to satisfy even the most demanding of customers. Choosing one of our products means choosing innovation and industrial processes that are based on cutting-edge technologies. Certina Tile has cemented its place in the tile market (single firing, double firing, porcelain stoneware, glazed stoneware) with its wide range of products that fully meet the customers’ requirements.

All of our designs represent a balanced mix of exceptional raw materials and innovative production techniques, to guarantee an exceptional end result in full respect of the surrounding environment. We believe that a focus on aesthetics and the skilful use of the most evolved technologies are the basis for the creation of premium quality products. Persian and Italian tradition, work culture, commitment to research and innovation, attention to aesthetic details: these are the values that set us apart and make Certina Tile an internationally recognised leader in the production of glazed and frost-resistant white glazed stoneware for floors and walls. A vast collection of Italian tiles for each and every type of project for cutting-edge tiling solutions for both indoor and outdoor environments. Glazed tiles, full body tiles, rectified tiles and in large sizes...Certina Tile, persian design and Italian made tiles only!

Technology

Certina has completely renovated its Solignano di Modena production facility. Built on a surface area of 100,000 sq m, this is currently one of the most modern, ecological and efficient ceramic production facilities in the World, able to produce over 7,000,000 sq m of ceramic slabs a year. Each production aspect has been optimized in terms of utmost energy output and minimum environmental impact. Products are made using cutting-edge technologies, such as inkjet digital decoration, firing in clean-energy heat-recycling kilns, “one roof” in-line grinding and polishing and electronic control of quality standards. The range of products adapts to all architectural projects, with over 20 interior and outdoor stone surfaces, more than 30 reinterpretations of the world’s most beautiful marbles; numerous 3-D relief surfaces evoking natural stone and its typical finishes, but also woods, resins and many other materials created for interior design. Numerous sizes are available in rectangular or square versions – from 30×30 cm to 120×240 cm – with a broad choice of ceramic slabs suitable for any project, public and private. Certina does in fact offer one of the largest product ranges in the industry today.
