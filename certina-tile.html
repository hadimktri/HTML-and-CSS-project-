we aim to be different

european and persian culture

Certina tile has been a presence on the international market since 2002 with a broad range of porcelain stoneware solutions for residential and project-planning destinations. Certina is part of the Gruppo Concorde, a leading ceramic group at global level. With everything we do, we aim to be different. We are constantly striving to put forth the very best product.

We offer the unique European and Persian aesthetic to homeowners and elopers with most of our selection imported directly from Iran and Italy. When it comes to commercial or residential projects, we’ve got you covered. Our sales team will help you use your budget efficiently to reach your design goals while providing all the technical assistance you need.

Twenty years of experience

Behind more than twenty years of experience and the constant desire to evolve for the future, meeting the influences of the home and the taste of an audience looking for desirable furnishing experiences and not just necessary objects. Our wide selection of porcelain stoneware floors and walls, sanitary wares, taps and accessories of all kinds give your living spaces a unique and exciting style.
